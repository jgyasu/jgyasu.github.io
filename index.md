# Jigyasu

I am an engineering undergraduate at the National Institute of Technology Agartala, interested in artificially intelligent systems.

Currently, I'm researching watermarking methods for AI-generated content in collaboration with AIISC, University of South Carolina, supervised by Dr. Amitava Das. 

I also contribute actively to sktime, a machine learning framework for time series. I was selected as a mentee and a member of the organisation. Spline trend forecaster is one of my contributions in the library and currently I am working to integrate TimeLLM in sktime.

I have spent most of my early university days with all kinds of clubs and societies and as a result, I am the vice president of the Data Science and AI Club and public relations lead for the SPIC MACAY heritage club.
