title: A Letter to The Future Self
---

Dear Jigyasu,

I hope you, the future me, are thriving in a life you envisioned for yourself. Don't worry, I won't be disappointed even if you have messed up somewhere in between, though I wanna know if you're still satisfied with yourself or not? 

Writing down this letter actually made me reflect on a lot of things in the present. I find myself navigating through a vast ocean of experiences and sometimes it feels like there is no pole star to guide me through. I guess this is how adulthood feels like, you're experiencing things that for you are very unique in their nature and somehow you are expected to get most of them right. You experience failures, success, love, loss, betrayal and all sorts of possible human emotions, and I suppose these experiences are not bad afterall, these are what makes you human and alive, no? 

More often than I would actually want it to happen, I find myself engulfed in a cloud of sadness and melancholy and you know what's cool about these clouds? The fact that they're clouds! They always float away, it's their very nature. I have started to identify these states of sadness and happiness as a group of clouds, they always float away and replace themselves. Sometimes it rains when it gets too heavy and at other times it's so warm and shiny that you could even feel the butterflies and birds rejoicing with you.

Jigyasu, I want you to know something, I don't know if you still are exactly the same in the way I feel things but I want you to be very grateful about people, things and experiences in life. You'd know of the lovely girl that I have because I like to believe that even years later we are still together, growing and thriving in love. I can only hope so but hope is a pretty pretty thing.

Someday I picture you sitting in a cafe somewhere in Vienna or Amsterdam watching the world go by as you read this letter addressed to you by me, the past you and while you do that, I want you to be very proud of yourself, you made it, bud! However, if you never get to experience this, I still want you to be equally proud, you tried your best.

I love you for not being an alpha, sigma or chad and just being the guy you're. Feel things, be vulnerable. Preserve your true self, the younger you finds it beautiful.

I wanna bid adieu here but before I end this, I wanna highlight the opening line of Billy Joel's Vienna, "Slow down you crazy child". Slow down somewhere down the line and be gentle with yourself.

With optimism and love,
Your Current Self

January 13th, 2024
